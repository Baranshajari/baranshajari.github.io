About Me

I am a graduate student studying computer science at McMaster University, with a focused interest in the Twin Transition, exploring the synergy between digitalization and sustainability. Currently, I am conducting research as a graduate student in the Sustainable Systems and Methods (SSM) Lab at McMaster, working on advancing knowledge and practical applications in this innovative field. Through my studies and lab experience, I aim to contribute to the intersection of technology and sustainable development.


When I am not studying, I enjoy playing and teaching piano: Piano YouTube Channel
